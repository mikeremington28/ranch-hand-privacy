Privacy Policy for Ranch Hand
Last updated: 12/31/2025
Ranch Hand respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Ranch Hand mobile application.
Information We Collect
Ranch Hand does not require you to create an account to use the app. Most data you enter (such as equipment and maintenance records) is stored locally on your device.
If you choose to create an optional account for backup and restore, we collect your email address for authentication purposes only.
How We Use Information
Collected information is used solely to:
Provide optional cloud backup and restore functionality


Authenticate your account if you choose to sign in


We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
Data Storage
Equipment and maintenance data is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, your data is securely stored and used only to restore your information.
Data Sharing
Ranch Hand does not share your personal data with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.
Your Choices
